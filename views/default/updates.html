Problem setters on StopStalk!

You can now see the problem setters of the problems across CodeChef, CodeForces, Spoj, HackerEarth, Hackerrank and Timus. You can also view the problems authored by a particular StopStalker on their profile page.

Updates in StopStalk Rating formula

We have updated our Rating formula to handle users submitting for same problem again and again. This will ensure fair leaderboards and not skew the numbers and make sure people stay motivated to code everyday on various different problems.

Editorial contributions to your Profile page

We are now also counting your contributions towards writing Editorials in the calendar graph of your profile page. More reasons to contribute to the community and have a deedy profile!

We have now added acm.timus.ru to StopStalk. Update your Timus ID on Update Details page to get your submissions from Timus.

Users can now write editorials

You can now write editorials for problems. Just open the problem page and click the Editorials button to contribute your great descriptive solutions to problems.

Heuristic submission retrieval

As we scale, we are making changes into our retrieval logic and making it more based on a heuristic instead of exhaustive retrieval. Checkout our exact algorithm here.

List of solved / unsolved problems of a user on Profile Page

Now you can see the exhaustive list of problems solved / attempted on the profile page and add them to your ToDo list or analyse your progress based on that which are also categorised with generalised tags.

Update in the rating formula

We have removed per day changes contribution to the rating and improved the formula to provide a more deedy number.

Instantly retrieve your submissions on StopStalk
